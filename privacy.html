Privacy Policy

Effective: June 30, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Override ("we," "our," or "us") handles your information when you use the Override mobile application and website at override-app.org (collectively, the "Service").

Override is used during sensitive personal moments. Privacy is not an afterthought — it is the core design principle of this app. Override is built to be local-first: your personal data stays on your device. We do not operate accounts, we do not run a backend server that stores your activity, and we do not sell your data.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

Override stores the following information locally on your device only. This data is not transmitted to us or to any server, and we cannot access it:

  • Your personal reasons: The reasons you enter during onboarding to remind yourself why you started.
  • Your clean timer and commitment data: Your start time and daily commitment status, used to display your progress.
  • Your final phrase: The personal phrase you write as the last step of an intervention.
  • Your Reset QR data: If you register a Reset QR code, the code's value is stored locally and used only to verify a match during a mission. It is never uploaded.
  • Your app settings and intervention state: Mission settings, shield configuration, and the status of any active intervention.

All of this data is stored in your device's local storage. It is removed when you delete the app or use the in-app reset option.

3. No Account, No Sign-In, No Tracking

Override does not require or offer account creation. There is no sign-in, no email collection, no user profile, and no cloud sync. We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising identifiers, or third-party trackers. Override's App Store privacy label reflects this as "Data Not Collected."

4. Screen Time & App Shielding (Family Controls)

Override uses Apple's Family Controls and Screen Time APIs to shield apps you select during an intervention you start yourself. Your app selection is handled entirely by Apple's system: it is stored on-device using opaque system tokens that Override cannot read, decode, or transmit. Override never sees which specific apps you choose — it only receives a count from the system. No app-usage data is collected, monitored, or uploaded. Shielding occurs only during interventions you initiate and is removed when the intervention completes or its safety window expires.

5. Camera

Some reset missions use your device camera — for example, scanning your registered Reset QR code, scanning a generated text code, recognizing a common object, or detecting an open palm. All camera processing happens on-device using Apple's Vision framework. Camera frames are processed in real time and are never stored, saved, or uploaded. No images leave your device.

6. Motion & Fitness

Some reset missions use your device's motion sensors — for example, the shake reset or a step-count mission. This motion data is read on-device only, used solely to complete the mission in the moment, and is never stored or transmitted.

7. Purchases

If you subscribe to Override Premium, your subscription is processed by Apple through the App Store and managed using RevenueCat, which handles subscription status and entitlement validation. Apple processes all payment information — we never see or store your payment card details. RevenueCat processes a non-personal, app-generated identifier and your transaction/subscription status in order to determine whether your access is active. We do not collect your name, email, or payment details through this process. RevenueCat's handling of this data is governed by their privacy policy.

8. Third-Party Services

Override relies on the following services in a limited capacity:

  • Apple App Store — processes all purchases and subscriptions, governed by Apple's privacy policy.
  • RevenueCat — manages subscription status and entitlements (revenuecat.com/privacy).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Override has no advertising partners.

9. Your Control Over Your Data

Because your data lives only on your device, you are in direct control of it:

  • Reset: You can clear your saved Override data at any time from within the app's settings.
  • Delete: Deleting the Override app removes all locally stored data from your device.
  • Subscriptions: You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your Apple ID settings.

If you have questions about your data, contact us at ask@override-app.org.

10. Children's Privacy

Override is rated for general audiences and is not directed at children under the age of 13. Because Override does not collect personal information, no personal data from any user — including children — is gathered by us. If you have questions, contact us at ask@override-app.org.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. If changes are material, we will reflect them here and in the app. Continued use of Override after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

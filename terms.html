5. Screen Time & Device Permissions

Override uses Apple's Family Controls and Screen Time APIs to shield apps you choose during an intervention you start yourself. Shielding occurs only during active interventions and is released when the intervention completes or its safety window expires. Override may also request access to your camera and motion sensors to complete reset missions; this processing happens entirely on your device. You may grant or deny these permissions in your device settings, though denying them may prevent certain missions from functioning.

The intervention safety window is managed by iOS and is intended to last approximately 15 minutes. Because expiry is system-managed, it should not be relied upon as exact to the second, and Override does not guarantee that shielding cannot be circumvented through device-level settings.
